School Sanggu Academic Subsidy (SAS) Program Applications

The Sanggunian Academic Subsidy program, or SAS for short, is a service of the Sanggunian that aims to strengthen the Sanggunian's relations with its students, and empower them by providing them financial support or the subsidization of their expenses for research, projects, theses, and any other major academic requirement.
