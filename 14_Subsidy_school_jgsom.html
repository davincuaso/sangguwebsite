JGSOM Academic Subsidy Application Kit

Application Guidelines

Description

The JGSOM Sanggunian Academic Subsidy System is a service of the JGSOM Sanggunian, in cooperation with the JGSOM Deanâ€™s Office. The subsidy system aims to empower the students by creating an avenue to help them develop their major academic projects such as theses and business research. Only a maximum of ten (10) groups will be subsidized.

Application Requirements

Any undergraduate student of the Loyola Schools is eligible to apply who is submitting their requirements in partial fulfillment of a major or minor in the John Gokongwei School of Management. The undergraduate student(s) must be enrolled in the class where the thesis/business research/project/paper is a requirement at the time of application.

For Scholars

Any applicant who is a scholar of any type, either applying as an individual or as a member of a group of applicants, must also individually fill out the Confidential Scholar Information Form and submit as a hard copy ONLY in a sealed envelope with their name, course, and title of said requirement. This document may be submitted either with the application kit or separately from the kit. Do NOT email this form.

Timeline

Application Period for JGSOM SAS: October 13 - November 22
Panel Interview: November 23 - December 9
Release of Results: December 11 - December 14
Note: Schedule may be subject to change depending on unforeseen events, number of applicants, and availability of panelists

Application Kit The JGSOM application kit contains:

  • Business Proposal
  • Budget Breakdown
  • Code of Accountability
  • Confidential Scholar's Information (for scholars only)
  • Miscellaneous Documents as deemed necessary
