Sabihin Sa Sanggu Student Rights and Welfare Desks (STRAWdesks)

The Resource Partnership Program is an initiative by the Sanggunian to provide support for organizations that create projects that raise awareness and contribute to nation building.

Concerns You Can Forward to Us

Academic Concerns

These are issues that are academic in nature; concerns about the curriculum, classes, professors, grades, and others related to these fall under this category.

Administrative/Facilities-Related Concerns

Issues with the facilities, services, and general operations of the Loyola Schools administration may be brought up as well.

Safety and Welfare Concerns

The Sanggunian may provide assistance in cases of student rights violations, whether peer-to-peer or against someone from the faculty/administration. These include but are not limited to: physical assault, sexual misconduct and discrimination/harassment.

Other

These are concerns that do not fall under any of the three categories.
